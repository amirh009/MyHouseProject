My Dynamic House!

 

This will only work in Firefox right now.

This example will only work if you have downloaded the processing.min.js file from processing.js

Here it is!

 

Hold F to make it turn to fall

Hold W to make it go to winter, all the way will cause a blizzard

Hold S to reset it to Spring/Summer

Use the arrow keys to move the alien

Click on the house to toggle the lights on and off

Click Shift to change color of house

Click Control to change color of the roof
