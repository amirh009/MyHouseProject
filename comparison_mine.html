The one on the left is my house and the one on the right is the other person's

-Apart from the black outlining, the houses are pretty much identical.

-Also, some of the coordinates are not exact because I used gridlines rather than exact coordinates.

-These were my instructions through paint:

  1. Click on view on the top and in the section of “show or hide”, check the box that says “Gridlines”
  2. In the shapes section click on the rectangle icon
  3. Click on color two then green and repeat with color one
  4. Click the fill bucket in shapes and click solid color
  5. Then make a green rectangle that fills the bottom half of the page
  6. Select blue ( the blue that is three from the right top row) for color 1 and color 2
  7. Make a blue rectangle that fills the top half of an image
  8. color 1 to black and color 2 to white
  9. make a large square in the middle of the screen, 29 pixels from the left, 29 pixels from the right, 12 pixels from the bottom and 10 pixels from the top
  10. make a smaller (8 pixels by 13 pixels) horizontal rectangle from the center bottom of the square (about 14 pixels from each side
  11. make two other small squares near the corners of the two top corners of the bigger square, each 6 x 8 pixels, each 5 pixels from the sides of the square and 5 from the top
  12. In shapes, click on the triangle
  13. Click and drag from the top left point to the top right point of the square then pull up about 7 pixels
  14. Click on the rectangle icon again in shapes
  15. On the right side of the triangle you just made, 3 pixels from the edge, make a vertical rectangle that is 7 x 9 pixels
  16. Click on the bucket in tools, then select the color red (forth from the top row left).
  17. Click on the big square so that it becomes red
  18. Then in the same way, make the rectangles (doors and mirrors) inside the square purple (top row last)
  19. Then in the same way, make the triangle and the chimney orange (top row, fifth)
  20. Click on the circle in shapes and draw a circle that is on the right side, 1 pixel from the top and 5 pixels from the right side of the screen
  21. Fill that circle in yellow ( top row sixth) in the way you did in steps 15, 17, and 18
  22. Click on the rectangle icon and on the left side create a 9 x 24 rectangle that is 6 pixels from the left side of the page and 16 pixels from the bottom
  23. Fill that rectangle with brown ( bottom row third) in the way u did before
  24. Finally, click on the circle icon in shapes and create an oval that is one pixel from the top of the image, two pixels from the left side of the image, and slightly overlaps the brown rectangle.
  25. Fill that in green ( top row, forth from right)
  26. Now you should have a red house with purple windows, an orange roof and chimney, a blue sky, a green yard, a tree and a sun.
